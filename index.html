Lion

A lion may sleep up to 20 hours a day.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Speed: 80 km/h
  • Average Lifespan: 10-14 years
  • Height: 1,2 m Male, 1.1 m Female

The lion is a species of carnivorous mammals of the felids, being one of the four species of the genus Panthera, a representative of the large feline subfamily. He is one of the biggest felines and one of the largest land-based rappers.
